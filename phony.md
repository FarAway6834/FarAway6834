## symbol definitions

notation)

for all natural number n, m, and for all syllable A₁, ..., Aₙ, B₁, ..., Bₘ, the n and m syllable word segment A₁…Aₙ, B₁…Bₘ is exist.
1. definition of insertion mark "ᵛ") for all 1 ≤ k < m, expression "B₁…BₖᵛB₍ₖ₊₁₎…Bₘ" compiles to phrase "B₁…Bₖ B₍ₖ₊₁₎…Bₘ"
2. definition of close up mark "⁀") expression "A₁…Aₙ⁀B₁…Bₘ" is compiles to word segment "A₁…AₙB₁…Bₘ"
As a result, the operation of correction signs for arbitrary syllables and word segment was defined.

symbol explanation)

in japanese, symbol "ー" compiles to triangular colon "ː" (only on japanese)

notation expensional bar)

as symbol "–", it's short "ー"
it means, "pronounce long, but don't increase the length of the entire pronunciation."

"–" > "-" and, it also "pronounce long, but don't increase the length of the entire pronunciation"

in IPA)
normal ending : ↘
global raise ending : ↗

normally, "AᵛB" is "A↘B".
but sometimes, it's "A↗B"

so...

define of carot expensional notation)

"A\^B" is compiles to "A↗B"
